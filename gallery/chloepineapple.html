Chloe Pineapple

In February 2018, my wife and I had to say goodbye to our beloved cat, Chloe. I painted this image and gave it to my wife as a Christmas present in December 2018. I wanted to choose a photo for the painting that was sortof funny, rather than sad, which is why I used this one with the pineapple.
