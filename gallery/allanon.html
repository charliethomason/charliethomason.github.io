Allanon

In 2018, I discovered the literary works of Terry Brooks, specifically his Shannara series. One of the most notable characters of this series is the druid Allanon. I painted this image using black India ink and a small brush, based on this illustration by Darrell K. Sweet from The Elfstones of Shannara.
