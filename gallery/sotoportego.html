Sotoportego

Part of a series of watercolor paintings of scenes of Venice, Italy, entitled L'Acqua di Venezia ('The Water of Venice'). Sotoportego is a uniquely Venetian term to describe an alley that passes underneath a building, typically near a bridge. This depicts the Ponte (bridge) dei Fuseri and a passing gondolier.
