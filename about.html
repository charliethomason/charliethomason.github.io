About Charlie

My name is Charlie Thomason and I'm a Software Engineer and Artist living in Chicago, Illinois. I love movies, auto racing, traveling, birds, playing guitar, jogging, tinkering with JavaScript, pizza, tacos, my wife Amanda, and our cat Baxter. I am currently a Sr. Software Engineer at Uptake, and was previously a Front-End Developer at Razorfish. I earned my BFA in Art Studio from the University of Kentucky, and my MA in New Media Studies from DePaul. This website features some of my best paintings, photography, and web development samples.

Some fun facts about me:

  • I met my wife, Amanda, at the 2009 Sundance Film Festival in Park City, Utah. We returned to the festival again in 2010, 2011, and 2015. We got engaged while at the 2015 festival.
  • I am a Catholic, and member at Old St. Pat's, which is where my wife and I were married in 2016.
  • I've enjoyed auto racing most of my life, and am a fan of Wood Brothers Racing, Team Penske, and Scuderia Ferrari.
  • I love sci-fi and fantasy novels, especially J.R.R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings and Terry Brooks' Shannara series.
  • My favorite places in the world are Venice, Italy and Emerald Isle, North Carolina.

About this website

The content on this site is all static HTML, built using Handlebars, Assemble, Grunt, and Sass. The JavaScript is all plain, vanilla JavaScript (except in certain code demos). The main site is hosted with Github Pages. If you want to see more of how the site is built, feel free to view the source code for this website.
